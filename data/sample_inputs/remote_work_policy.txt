Northbridge Softworks — Remote Work Policy
Effective Date: January 1, 2026
Version: 2.1

1. Purpose
This policy defines the rules for employees who work remotely, either full-time
or in a hybrid arrangement, at Northbridge Softworks.

2. Eligibility
Employees are eligible for remote work after completing 90 days of continuous
employment and receiving written approval from their direct manager. Interns and
contractors on assignments shorter than 3 months are not eligible for full-time
remote arrangements but may work remotely up to 2 days per week with manager approval.

3. Work Hours and Availability
Remote employees must be reachable on company communication tools (Slack and email)
between 10:00 AM and 4:00 PM in their local time zone, regardless of their broader
working hours. Core meeting attendance is mandatory unless the employee has
pre-approved leave.

4. Equipment
Northbridge Softworks provides a laptop, external monitor, and a one-time home
office stipend of $400 USD for employees approved for full-time remote work.
Employees are responsible for maintaining a stable internet connection of at
least 25 Mbps download / 5 Mbps upload.

5. Security Requirements
All remote employees must enable full-disk encryption, use the company VPN when
accessing internal systems, and complete the annual security awareness training.
Company devices may not be used by anyone other than the employee, including
family members.

6. Expense Reimbursement
Reasonable home-office expenses (chair, desk, monitor arm) up to $150 USD per
year may be reimbursed with receipts submitted through the finance portal within
30 days of purchase. Internet and electricity bills are not reimbursed.

7. Performance Reviews
Remote employees are evaluated using the same performance framework as in-office
staff. Managers are expected to conduct at least one video check-in per week with
each direct report working remotely.

8. Policy Review
This policy is reviewed annually by the People Operations team. The next scheduled
review is January 2027. Questions about this policy should be directed to
hr@northbridgesoftworks.example.
